## العربية (ar) — "الجديد في هذا الإصدار"

كابتن حمودي صار يتذكّر محادثتك كاملة، مهما طالت.

• المحادثات الطويلة قبل كانت "تنسى" بدايتها — هسة الكابتن يطوي أول المحادثة بذاكرة أمينة (هدفك، النقاط اللي ذكرتها، والخطط والوعود اللي عطاك ياها) ويكمل وياك بنفس الشاشة بدون ما يضيع الخيط.
• الملخّص مأخوذ من كلامك الفعلي — مو مخترَع — والكابتن مُوجَّه إنه ما يخمّن أي تفصيل مو موجود.
• يطلعلك سطر لطيف "طويت بداية محادثتنا بذاكرتي" حتى تشوف إنه ما نسى شي.
• كروت خطط التمرين صارت تظهر كاملة داخل الدردشة، يوم بيوم.
• تحسينات أداء وثبات: تشغيل أنعم على الأجهزة الأقل، ومعالجة أذكى وقت الضغط.

تحليل النوم يضل يشتغل على جهازك بالكامل. خصوصيتك أولاً.

## English (en) — "What's New"

Captain Hamoudi now remembers your whole conversation, no matter how long it runs.

• Long chats used to lose their beginning. Now the Captain folds the earlier part of the chat into a faithful memory — your goal, the points you made, and the plans and promises it already gave you — and continues in the same conversation without losing the thread.
• The summary is built from what was actually said, never invented, and the Captain is told not to guess any detail it doesn't have.
• A soft "earlier chat folded into memory" marker shows you nothing was dropped.
• Workout plans now render as full day-by-day cards right inside the chat.
• Performance & stability hardening: smoother on lower-end devices and smarter handling under load.

Sleep analysis still runs fully on your device. Privacy first.

## Reviewer notes (App Review → "Notes")

- No new data types collected, no new permissions, no new third-party SDKs vs. 1.0.5.
- The conversation-memory feature is derived only from chat content that already goes to the AI service, and passes through the same on-device PII-redaction + health-number bucketing before any network send. Sleep analysis remains fully on-device.
- AI cloud features require the existing one-time AI Data consent; the medical/wellness disclaimer banner is unchanged. The Captain is a wellness coach, not a medical provider.
- Test account / demo: same as 1.0.5 submission (no change). All AI features are reachable on the Max tier; a reviewer demo entitlement is available on request.
